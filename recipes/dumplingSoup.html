Dumpling Soup

Description

This is an amazing dish that can be created in thirty minutes. It consists of a delicious dumpling in a hot broth. It swims in a medley of vegetables and seasoning.

While the water is coming to boil, prepare all the vegetables. Once it is boiling, add the seasoning and the vegetables. Then, you can add the frozen dumplings

Ingredients

  • Frozen dumplings
  • Onion
  • Chicken Boullion

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add vegetables and seasoning
  3. Add dumplings
