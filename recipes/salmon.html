Salmon Recipe

Description

This is an amazing dish that can be created in under an hour. After heavily seasoning the salmon with the ingredients below, you want to place the salmon in a searing pan with olive oil. Cook the salmon one on side until the side of the salmon is cooked halfway. Then flip it.

While the salmon is cooking, you should prepare your asparagus to save time. When the salmon is done, set it aside and cook the asparagus in the residule salmon oil. Season with oyster sauce and in a couple minutes it is done.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Asparagus
  • Oyster Sauce
